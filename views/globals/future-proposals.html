Project Proposals for next students

Names Popularity

Compare how popular names were around a certain time and location or check how names gained popularity based on famous people achievements.

Catalan Portal

Create a FamilySearch search portal in Catalan that also localizes the returned data from the API. It can also be applied to other languages!

Social Media

This functionality joins the FamilySearch data with the main social networks, providing funny comparisons between the users and their friends.

Genealogy & Heraldy

This project aims to reunite the Genealogy and Heraldy sciences, two sciences that were almost the same on their origins.

Indexing Projects

Leaving the API aside for a second, these ideas aim to help the FamilySearch organization by automating parts of the registers indexing process.

LDS Church

Study the history and expansion of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints through the genealogical data accessible from their API.

Ancestry Diversity

The world we live in is actually pretty global. This feature aims to expose how rich each surname is in terms of races and ethnicities that have it.

Collections

Given the input of a location and a period of time, list all the available collections on the FamilySearch API that contain related data.

WW2: Natality & Mortality

One of the conflicts that altered the world natality and mortality ratios was the Second World War. This projects studies it.

WW2: Marriages

When the United States joined the WW2, a huge increase on the number of marriages was registered. Can we see that effect on the FamilySearch data?

Schindler's List

Based on the surnames of those that formed the Schindler's List, study what happened to the Jews Collective during and after the Second World War.

Great Depression

Another historical event that had a huge impact on the United States and also worldwide, was the Great Depression. This project studies its effects.

Surnames Geolocation

Improve the surname geolocation feature developed on this website by expanding it to other region levels.

Reality vs FS

Study the statistical accuracy of the accessible data through the FS API by comparing it with real reports based on genealogical data.

Genealogies

This project aims to study the Genealogies contained on the FamilySearch API and study their main characteristics and differences over time.

Duplicates

The Duplicates project idea tries to understand how FS marks persons as a duplicate and see if the algorithm could be improved on some cases.
